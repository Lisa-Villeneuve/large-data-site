Quartz Spessartine Feldspar

Quartz

Color: Colorless through various colors to black

Crystal habit: 6-sided prism ending in 6-sided pyramid (typical), drusy, fine-grained to microcrystalline, massive

Spessartine

Color: Yellow through red

Crystal habit: Massive to crystalline

Feldspar

Color: Pink, white, gray, brown

Crystal habit: Triclinic or monoclinic
