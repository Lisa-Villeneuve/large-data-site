About Us

GeoClassify is an educational resource that can aid you in identifying your specimens at a general level and specific level. Or if you just want to learn about the main types of rocks and minerals and see specific examples from a collection of beautiful specimens, this website is organized to easy navigation. This website is adaptable to any screen size and can be easily printed out. We hope that you enjoy your visit and we wish you luck in classifying you specimens.
